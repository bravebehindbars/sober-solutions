About Addiction...

Substance abuse encompasses the detrimental or risky use of psychoactive substances, such as alcohol and illicit drugs, leading to addiction, health-related issues, and various social and economic repercussions. When initiating the journey towards recovery, acknowledging the presence of the issue and seeking assistance constitute pivotal first steps. This may entail engaging with healthcare professionals, participating in support groups, undergoing therapeutic interventions, or enrolling in rehabilitation programs. It is imperative to recognize that the path to recovery is a gradual process, and garnering support from individuals within one's social circle as well as professionals can significantly facilitate this pursuit.
